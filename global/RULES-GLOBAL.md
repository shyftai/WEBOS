# Content Rules — Global

These rules apply to ALL workspaces. They cannot be overridden.

## Quality standards

1. **No fabrication** — never invent statistics, quotes, or sources. If you can't verify it, don't include it.
2. **Attribution required** — every claim needs a source. Link to the original.
3. **Plagiarism zero tolerance** — all content must be original. Inspiration is fine, copying is not.
4. **Brand consistency** — every piece must pass a brand voice check before approval.
5. **Accessibility** — all content should be readable at an 8th-grade level unless the brief specifies otherwise.

## SEO standards

1. **No keyword stuffing** — keywords guide structure, never forced into prose
2. **Search intent first** — match the content type to the search intent (informational, navigational, transactional)
3. **E-E-A-T compliance** — demonstrate Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness
4. **Technical SEO basics** — every page needs: meta title (≤60 chars), meta description (≤155 chars), H1, proper heading hierarchy

## Publishing standards

1. **Brief required** — no content published without an approved brief
2. **Review required** — no content published without at least one quality gate pass
3. **Calendar compliance** — respect the content calendar; don't publish ad hoc without good reason
4. **Cross-channel coordination** — when repurposing, publish the primary piece first, then derivatives

## Compliance

1. **Copyright** — respect copyright law. Don't reproduce copyrighted content without permission.
2. **Disclosure** — disclose sponsored content, affiliate links, and AI-assisted writing where required by law
3. **Privacy** — don't include PII in public content
4. **Platform rules** — follow each platform's content guidelines and terms of service

## Measurement

1. **Track against brief** — measure performance against the brief's success metric
2. **No vanity metrics** — impressions without engagement context are meaningless
3. **Attribution matters** — track which content drives actual business outcomes
4. **Report honestly** — if content underperformed, say so. Learnings come from honesty.
