# Content Benchmarks

## Blog / Website

| Metric | Poor | Average | Good | Excellent |
|--------|------|---------|------|-----------|
| Organic traffic growth (MoM) | <2% | 2-5% | 5-10% | >10% |
| Avg time on page | <1 min | 1-2 min | 2-4 min | >4 min |
| Bounce rate | >70% | 50-70% | 35-50% | <35% |
| Pages per session | <1.5 | 1.5-2.5 | 2.5-4 | >4 |
| Blog conversion rate | <0.5% | 0.5-1% | 1-3% | >3% |

## SEO

| Metric | Poor | Average | Good | Excellent |
|--------|------|---------|------|-----------|
| Domain authority | <20 | 20-40 | 40-60 | >60 |
| Keywords in top 10 | <50 | 50-200 | 200-1000 | >1000 |
| Organic CTR (avg) | <1% | 1-3% | 3-5% | >5% |
| Page 1 ranking rate | <10% | 10-25% | 25-50% | >50% |
