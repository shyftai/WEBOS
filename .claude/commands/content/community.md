<objective>
Create content for community engagement. Not broadcast — conversation.

Workspace: $ARGUMENTS
</objective>

<process>
1. Display mode header: `<< CONTENT:OS // COMMUNITY >>`
2. Load workspace: BRAND.md, AUDIENCE.md, TOV.md, CHANNELS.md, LEARNINGS.md
3. Ask platform and mode:
   - **Answer:** craft helpful responses to community questions
   - **Start discussion:** create engaging discussion prompts
   - **Share:** adapt content for community sharing (not promotion)
   - **Engage:** craft comments and replies for ongoing threads

4. Rules for community content:
   - Never be promotional — add value first
   - Match the community's tone, not just your brand tone
   - Lead with expertise, not product
   - Disclose affiliation where required
   - Be genuinely helpful

5. Generate content with community context
6. Display for review
7. Save to content/approved/
</process>
