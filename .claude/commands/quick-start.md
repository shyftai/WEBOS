"What does your company do, and who is it for?"

## Block 2 — Audience
"Who reads your content? (role, industry, awareness level)"

Save to: `AUDIENCE.md`

## Block 3 — Channels
"Where do you publish? (blog, landing pages, docs, knowledge base)"

Save to: `CHANNELS.md`

## Block 4 — Voice
"How should your content sound? (professional, casual, technical, playful)"

Save to: `TOV.md`

## Block 5 — Goals
"What's your #1 content goal? (traffic, leads, brand awareness)"

Save to: `ROADMAP.md`
