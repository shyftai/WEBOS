# Distribution Playbook

## The 80/20 rule of content
20% of effort creating. 80% distributing. Most people get this backwards.

## Distribution tiers

### Tier 1: Owned channels (free, immediate)
- Company blog/website
- Email newsletter
- Social media profiles
- Community presence (own Discord/Slack)
- YouTube channel

### Tier 2: Earned distribution (free, requires effort)
- SEO (organic search traffic)
- Social shares and engagement
- Backlinks from other sites
- PR and media mentions
- Guest posts
- Podcast appearances
- Community engagement (Reddit, forums)

### Tier 3: Paid amplification (cost per click/impression)
- Social ads (LinkedIn, Meta, Twitter)
- Search ads (Google, Bing)
- Sponsored newsletters
- Content syndication
- Influencer partnerships

## Repurposing waterfall

```
    LONG-FORM CONTENT (blog/guide/video)
              │
    ┌─────────┼─────────┬──────────┐
    │         │         │          │
  Social    Thread   Newsletter  Slides
  posts     (X/LI)   feature    (deck)
    │                    │
  Stories              Community
  /Reels               post
```

1. Start with the deepest version (long-form)
2. Extract key points → social posts
3. Pull the narrative → thread
4. Feature in newsletter
5. Convert to slides/carousel
6. Adapt for community sharing
7. Create short-form video script

## Distribution timeline

| Timing | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Day 0 | Publish primary piece |
| Day 0-1 | Share across owned social channels |
| Day 1 | Send in newsletter (if applicable) |
| Day 1-3 | Share in relevant communities |
| Day 3-7 | Repurpose into thread/carousel |
| Day 7-14 | Syndicate or pitch for guest post |
| Week 3-4 | Refresh and reshare with new angle |
| Month 2-3 | Update with new data, republish |

## Amplification tactics

- **Employee advocacy:** team members share (biggest LinkedIn hack)
- **Engagement pods:** coordinate with peers for initial boost (use carefully)
- **Cross-promotion:** partner with complementary brands
- **Influencer seeding:** share with influencers before publishing
- **Community first:** test ideas in communities before publishing
